Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-10-30

Daily Quote fetches quote data from a remote JSON hosted by the developer to display daily content. The app may request network access to download these quotes.

The app stores any quotes you mark as favorites locally on your device only (in the app's private storage). Favorites are not uploaded or shared with any third party.

No personal data (name, email, device identifiers) is collected, transmitted, or sold. The app does not use analytics or advertising SDKs.
